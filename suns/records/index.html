                                        Suri Records

                                        # Suri Records

                                        Suri records are the minimum source of information of a suri. Each record contains a protocol stack. The protocol stack itself stores the necessary data to resolve the suri for a specific protocol or protocols and get their value.

                                        All records are stored on-chain, can be accessed by anyone and are managed by its suri's owner.
