                                        # ENS - Ethereum Name Service

                                        ENSopen in new window is a decentralized naming system based on the Ethereum blockchain. It is famous for providing access to .eth domains. The main advantage of ENS is to be based on ethereum, one of the most used blockchain networks, but that comes along with its main drawback: any action on your domain or records implies to pay a great fee.
